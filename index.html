Who We Are

A Group Built
to Last Decades.

M.A.G.K Groups is a registered, multi-discipline construction and technology group headquartered in Nyagatare, Eastern Province, Rwanda.

We were built with a single conviction: that Eastern Rwanda deserves the same standard of construction professionalism found in Kigali and beyond.

Our group structure spans construction, technology, materials, and advisory — designed to grow alongside Rwanda's Vision 2050 and the East Africa construction boom.
