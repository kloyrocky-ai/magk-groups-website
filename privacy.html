01

Overview

M.A.G.K Groups ("we", "our", "us") is a registered construction and technology company based in Nyagatare, Eastern Province, Rwanda. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our clients, website visitors, and business partners.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms described here.

Simple version: We collect only what we need to serve you. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with anyone who does not need it to help run your project.

02

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways — information you give us directly, and basic information collected automatically when you use our website.

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, phone number, service requested, project location, and description
  • Client portal registration — your email address used to access project tracking
  • Direct communications — messages sent via WhatsApp, email, or phone
  • Project information — details about your construction project provided during consultation

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic usage data — pages visited, time spent on the site (via Google Search Console only)
  • Device information — browser type and general location region for improving site performance

We do not use tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or behavioral profiling tools on this website.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes only:

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a project quotation
  • To manage your construction project and keep you updated on progress
  • To give you access to the client portal so you can track your project
  • To send you project-related documents such as contracts, estimates, and milestone reports
  • To contact you about your project via phone, WhatsApp, or email
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations in Rwanda

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to your project or enquiry.

04

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service delivery — with subcontractors or suppliers only when strictly necessary to complete your project, and only the information they need
  • Legal compliance — when required by Rwandan law, court order, or government authority
  • Technology partners — our website uses Supabase for data storage (see Third-Party Services section)

Any party with whom we share your data is bound by confidentiality obligations. Your project information is never shared publicly.

05

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a professional database platform with industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Data is stored on servers within the EU region.

We apply the following security measures:

  • Row-level security policies — each client only sees their own project data
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) on all pages
  • Password-protected admin access — only authorized M.A.G.K Groups personnel can access your data
  • Regular access reviews to ensure only necessary personnel have access

We retain your data for as long as necessary to complete your project and fulfill any legal obligations. If you request deletion of your data, we will action it within 30 days.

06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction — you can ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Right to deletion — you can request that we delete your personal information
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time
  • Right to object — you can object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking cookies, or any cookie-based behavioral profiling.

The only cookies we use are:

  • Authentication cookies — used by our client portal to keep you logged in during your session. These are deleted when you log out or close your browser.
  • Google Fonts — a third-party font service that may set a performance cookie. No personal data is collected through this.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies will prevent the client portal from functioning correctly.

08

Third-Party Services

Our website and systems use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database and authentication platform. Stores enquiry forms and client project data. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — website hosting platform. Processes basic web traffic. Netlify Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — font rendering service. No personal data collected.
  • WhatsApp Business — direct client communication. Subject to Meta's privacy policy.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising or retargeting platforms.

09

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults and businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
